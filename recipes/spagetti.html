Tomato and Mozarella Salad

Ingredients

  • Spagetti
  • Olive Oil
  • Garlic
  • Small Tomatoes
  • Parsley, Salt, Pepper
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Seedless Black Olives
  • Anchovy Fillet
  • Hot Pepper

The origin of the name supposedly comes from a prosistute that first made the dish out of random or worthless ingredients

Fun fact: The Italian word for prostitute is 'puttana', while the word for useless is 'puttana'
